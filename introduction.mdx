## Key Features

1. **Multiple Currencies:** Create and track multiple currencies for each member of your Discord server.
![Demonstrates creation of multiple currencies](https://imgur.com/yQcVepJ.png)
2. **Advanced Shops:** Customizable shops built directly within Discord's forums channel, offering unique buying experiences.
![Demonstrates advanced forum shops](https://imgur.com/hoH9AgX.png)
![Demonstrates advanced use of a shop showing the ability to sell a role for a timed amount (x days) after which it will be removed](https://imgur.com/LLKi8yk.png)
3. **Custom Commands:** Fully customizable commands with any trigger to automate currency actions seamlessly.
4. **Companies and Corporations:** Enable users to create shared bank accounts using companies.
5. **In-Game Economies:** Build economies from famous games such as Clash of Clans and Rome 2 directly into Discord.
![Demonstrates economies from famous games](https://imgur.com/JnArBDT.png)
6. **Virtualize In-Game Currencies:** People can virtualize their in-game currencies to Discord such as ARK BBS and Minecraft Diamonds.
![Demonstrates virtualizing in-game currencies](https://imgur.com/P3EOttq.png)
7. **Spread Across Servers:** You can spread your economy to multiple servers.
![Demonstrates spreading economy to multiple servers](https://imgur.com/C0w1Df0.png)

## Perfect For

- **In-Game Goods:** Digitize and manage in-game currencies like Minecraft diamonds or ARK berry bush seeds.
- **Roleplay:** Enhance roleplay scenarios by tracking items like armies, spells, and potions.
- **Fun:** Create and maintain a complex economic system purely for fun.
- **Rewards:** Track rewards and donations given to your users, enhancing engagement and participation.
